What I want

I want to discuss the state of the world, it's problems and possible solutions. Any and all.

I want it to be succinct. Speed beats everything else. Clarity. Truth. Notation.

I want it to be reasonably extremist and approach middle ground from there.

I want it to be as simply phrased and explainable as possible.

I want the discussion to happen in this format, or another one that's functionally the same. I don't care about the platform, I care about the methods. I think something like this format is necessary.

The core problem is utopia. Pick your version, think about it, think of the requirements to make it happen and we'll see what we can do.

Problems on the path to achieving it are sure to include issues like violence, inequality, freedom, justice and all the usual suspects.
