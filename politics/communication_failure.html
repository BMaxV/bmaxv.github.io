Communication Failure

If I make an argument, in a language you don't speak, you will fail to understand it.

Is that your fault for not knowing the language, or mine for not accomodating you?

[l]Superiority

If what you want to gain from an exchange is a feeling of superiority, you can have that.

E.g. "[math] q.e.d. I got mine, if you don't get it, that's your problem."

[l]Compromise and cooperation

If what you want to gain from an exchange is cooperation and help through insight, you need to make a good, working appeal, and the other person needs to agree with you.

E.g. "If you don't get it, it's our problem."

[l]Overruling

If what you're doing is merely a hopefully informative or official statement informing other people of your intention, action or reasoning, without the need for feedback... Don't be surprised if they don't react nicely.

E.g. "As your ruler, I am informing you of our action X, your understanding or coopration is not required."
