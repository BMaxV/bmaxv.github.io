politics

"politics" usually describes a narrow set of social interactions. I want it to be used in the broadest possible sense. In greek it was about "the affairs of the people of the city", which also probably meant the rich, ruling class.

I want to use it as "issues of or with people".

Politics is what happens between people
The topics of politics are generally about who is allowed or forbidden
  • to be somewhere
  • to do something
  • to claim something and to have society back that claim
