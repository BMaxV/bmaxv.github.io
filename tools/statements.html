statements

Something that can be "seen" is that, making really good statements or speeches or texts is really really hard. I think it's generally underestimated just how hard it is. It is this difficult because being succint and detailed enough at the same time, requires a lot of thinking about word choice and what actually is the thing to be said.

I made this data format to split things up and really look at them in detail.

https://github.com/BMaxV/discussion_bit

The idea being that, since statements are composed of smaller statements as I wrote about in the logic section, individual parts of the statements can and should be isolated.
