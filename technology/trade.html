trade

one of the most basic concepts and one of the most important ones, is trade/cooperation. I don't mean formalized trade, but the basic tribal behavior, that we can find in animals, like apes but also swarm insects.

its usually a very optimistic thing. it doesnt necessarily need accounting or contracts, it works on a unspoken "favor" basis as well.

Understanding this is the prerequisite to understanding scamming.
