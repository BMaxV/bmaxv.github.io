explicit vs. implicit

You can write things that are coincidentally conforming to a standard like 2024 12 24 describing the 2rth of decemver accirding to the iso 8601 standard. Or we can write things explicitly. 

hello this the 2024-12-24 and we are celebrating christmas.

and while it is slightly more effort to do this, it enhances the quality of the text and the data a lot. theoretically we could now interpret things client side and display the date however we want.

obviously this is currently not being done.
